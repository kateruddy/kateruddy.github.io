About Me

In fun-fact format

I spent my high school years at Walnut Hill School for the Arts, a private arts training institution in Natick, MA. After a full day of algebra, English, and chemistry classes, I spent my afternoons learning how to sing, dance, and act, followed by rehearsals in the evenings. If you've seen Fame, it was a lot like that.

I have my black belt in Krav Maga, which is Israeli fighting defense. From the ages of six to about twelve, I went to class a couple times a week. I can't remember much of it now, but I do still have the belt!

During my late high school and early college years, I worked as a professional actor! I did a lot of auditions and a few projects, including the independent film pictured here and an Indian pop music video.
